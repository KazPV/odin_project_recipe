Potato Chip Fried Rice (from "Weathering With You")

Introduction

The topic is boiling! Introducing a recipe that reproduces the salad dish in "Weathering with You", at the comfort of your home.

How about a salad that is easy to make, with a crunchy texture and perfect for hospitality? Please wear your favorite dressing and enjoy it. May it be a wonderful recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 200g of rice
  • 15g of potato chips (preferably salt and seaweed)
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • 1/4 pack of sprouts
  • 1 tsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of chicken stock conentration
  • 1/2 tsp of grated ginger
  • 1 tblsp of sesame oil
  • 1 egg
