Potato Chip Fried Rice (from "Weathering With You")

Introduction

The topic is boiling! Introducing a recipe that reproduces the salad dish in "Weathering with You", at the comfort of your home.

How about a salad that is easy to make, with a crunchy texture and perfect for hospitality? Please wear your favorite dressing and enjoy it. May it be a wonderful recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 200g of rice
  • 15g of potato chips (preferably salt and seaweed)
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • 1/4 pack of sprouts
  • 1 tsp of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of chicken stock conentration
  • 1/2 tsp of grated ginger
  • 1 tblsp of sesame oil
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Prepare the vegetables by chopping the onion and cutting off the stem from the sprouts. Chop sprouts into 2cm widths.
  2. Heat frying pan on low with sesame oil and ginger. Then add onions. Raise to medium heat and cook onions until translucent.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce and half of the sprouts and continue to cook.
  4. Crush potato chips by hand and mix into dish, then quickly remove from heat and serve.
  5. Separate egg yolk from whites. Create dimple in rice and add egg yolk.

Cooking tips:

Adjust the amount of salt to your liking.

Although the finished rice is cold and soldery, it can be made delicious even with warm rice.

Avoid eating raw eggs if you are elderly, infants under 2 years old, pregnant women, or those with weakened immune system.
